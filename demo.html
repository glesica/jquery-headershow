jQuery-headershow Demo Page

This page demonstrates a simple use of jQuery-headershow.

I looked at a bunch of plugins and tools for creating this sort of slide show. Many of them are really neat, but they didn't do exactly what I wanted.

You can also let the original content remain visible, just set hidecontent to false (see page source).

This would allow you to feature information certain pieces of information in an attractive manner but still let users view all of it at once.

The actual file names of the images do not matter one bit. The content is pulled straight from the DOM.

You can even include things like forms!
